# Feature Tour

Magic Feed is a rich content sharing app built with Flutter. It takes any web link and turns it into something worth keeping and worth sharing — a rich preview card, a designed poster, a QR code, or a collection of downloaded images.

## Capture links from anywhere

There are four ways to get a link into Magic Feed:

- **Share from another app** — Magic Feed registers as a share target, so you can send a page to it straight from your browser or any other app.
- **Paste from the clipboard** — one tap shares whatever URL you copied last.
- **Type or paste a URL** — a simple input page for entering any web address.
- **Scan a QR code** — use the camera (with flash toggle) or decode a QR code from an image in your gallery.

App shortcuts on your launcher jump straight to *Scan QR code* and *Share from clipboard*.

## Rich page previews

When you add a link, Magic Feed fetches the page and extracts its **title, description, and images** using Open Graph and HTML metadata. If a fetch needs a specific browser identity, you can set a **custom User-Agent** in Settings.

## Your feed

Saved links appear in a chronological feed you can actually work with:

- **Search** across titles and descriptions.
- **Bookmark** links to keep them handy.
- **List or grid layout**, switchable with one tap.
- **Infinite scrolling** through your history.
- Open any link in the **in-app WebView** or the **system browser via Custom Tabs** — your choice in Settings.

## Share cards & posters

The signature feature: turn any saved link into a designed poster.

- **Multiple card styles** — swipe the card to switch between designs, including **Modern** and **Liquid Glass**.
- **Image treatment** — choose between a cropped cover or the full image.
- **Composable content** — include or exclude the title, description, and cover image.
- **QR code included** — every poster carries a scannable "SCAN TO OPEN" code.
- **Save or share** the finished card as an image, or generate a standalone QR code instead.

## Images & gallery

- **Media list** — see every image a page exposes, multi-select, and download in bulk.
- **Custom download location** — pick any folder; Magic Feed remembers it.
- **Gallery tab** — browse everything you've downloaded, search your media, switch to list view, and open images in a **fullscreen viewer** with pinch-to-zoom.

## Backup & restore

Your feed is yours to keep:

- **Local backups** — create one anytime, and choose a backup folder outside app storage so backups **survive uninstall**.
- **Export & import** — move backup files between devices.
- **Auto backup** — automatically back up to local storage on changes.
- **Google Drive cloud backup** — sign in with Google and Magic Feed keeps your **10 most recent backups** in the cloud, restorable from any device.

## Made to fit you

- **Color modes** — system, light, or dark.
- **8 languages** — English, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Japanese, Korean, and Chinese (or follow the system default).
- **Adaptive Material 3 UI** — a bottom navigation bar on phones that becomes a navigation rail on larger screens.
- **Shared text options** — control whether the page title and description are included when you share a link as text.
