# Images & Gallery

Magic Feed collects the images from every page you save and gives you tools to browse and download them.

## Browse a page's media

From a feed item, choose **View media** to open the media list — every image the page exposed, in one grid.

- **Select images** — tap to multi-select exactly the ones you want.
- **Download** — grab your selection in one go; Magic Feed reports how many images were saved.
- **Fullscreen preview** — open any image in the viewer before deciding.

If a page doesn't expose any downloadable images, Magic Feed says so instead of showing an empty grid.

## Choose where downloads go

By default images are saved to your Pictures folder. In [Settings](./settings#downloads) you can **choose any folder** — Magic Feed remembers it and keeps saving there. If access to the folder is ever lost (for example after system cleanup), the app tells you and helps you re-select it.

## The Gallery tab

Everything you download lands in the **Gallery** tab:

- **Grid and list views**, switchable with one tap.
- **Search your media** by name.
- **Fullscreen viewer** with pinch-to-zoom and swipe navigation.
- **Share or delete** images directly from the gallery.

The gallery reads from your download folder, so images saved by Magic Feed are always in your device's photo library too — never locked inside the app.
