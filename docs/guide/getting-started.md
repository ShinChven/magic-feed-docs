# Getting Started

Magic Feed is a Flutter app for saving, enriching, and sharing web links. This guide walks you through the basics.

## The app at a glance

Magic Feed has four main tabs:

| Tab | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Feed** | Your saved links, searchable and bookmarkable |
| **Gallery** | Images you've downloaded from pages |
| **Backups** | Local and cloud backups of your feed |
| **Settings** | Appearance, language, downloads, and more |

On phones these appear as a bottom navigation bar; on tablets and wide screens they move to a navigation rail.

## Save your first link

1. Open the **Feed** tab and tap **Add**.
2. Choose how to add a link:
   - **Enter a URL** — paste or type a web address.
   - **Share from clipboard** — use the URL you copied last.
   - **Scan QR code** — with the camera or from an image.
3. Magic Feed fetches the page's title, description, and images, then shows you a share sheet with everything it found.

You can also share a page **directly from another app** — pick *Add to Magic Feed* in your system share menu.

## What you can do with a saved link

From any item in your feed:

- **Open it** in the in-app browser or Custom Tabs.
- **Share the link** as text, with optional title and description.
- **Generate a poster or QR code** — see [Share Cards & QR Codes](./share-cards).
- **View media** — browse and download the page's images.
- **Bookmark**, **copy the link**, **add to calendar**, or **delete** it.
