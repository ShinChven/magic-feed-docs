# Backup & Restore

Your feed is yours to keep. The **Backups** tab manages local backup files and Google Drive cloud backups side by side.

## Local backups

- **New local backup** — snapshot your entire feed to a file, anytime.
- **Backup details** — see each backup's file name, modification date, size, and how many feeds it contains.
- **Restore** — bring back every feed from any backup file.
- **Delete** — remove backup files you no longer need.

### Export & import

- **Export backup…** — save a backup file anywhere, or send it to another device.
- **Import backup…** — pick a backup file and restore your feed from it. Perfect for moving to a new phone.

### Backups that survive uninstall

By default backups live in app storage. In [Settings](./settings#data-management) you can **choose a backup folder** outside the app — backups go to `<folder>/magic_feed_backup` and **survive uninstalling the app**.

### Auto backup

Turn on **Auto backup to local storage** and Magic Feed keeps your backup current without you thinking about it.

## Google Drive cloud backup

Sign in with Google on the **Cloud** tab to back up to Google Drive:

- **Backup to Cloud** — upload a backup with one tap.
- **Auto backup to Google Drive** — back up automatically as your feed changes.
- **10 most recent backups** are kept safely in the cloud.
- **Restore from any device** — sign in on a new phone and pull your feed down.

Backups are stored in your own Google Drive account. You can sign out at any time; your cloud backups simply stop syncing.

::: tip Session expired?
If a cloud operation fails because your Google session expired, just use **Sign in again** — your backups are untouched.
:::
