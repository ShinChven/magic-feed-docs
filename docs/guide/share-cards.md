# Share Cards & QR Codes

Magic Feed's signature feature: turn any saved link into a designed poster or a scannable QR code.

## Generate a poster

From a feed item or the share sheet, choose **Poster / QR code**. Magic Feed composes a share card from the page's metadata.

### Swipe to change style

The poster page holds multiple card designs — **swipe the card left or right** to switch between them:

- **Modern** — a clean, bold layout with the cover image, title, and description.
- **Liquid Glass** — a frosted, translucent design with the cover image glowing through.

Magic Feed remembers the style you used last.

### Customize the card

- **Image treatment** — switch between **Crop** (a tidy cover) and **Full** (the entire image).
- **Include title** — show or hide the page title.
- **Include description** — show or hide the description.
- **Include cover image** — show or hide the image entirely.

Card colors adapt to the cover image thanks to automatic palette extraction.

### Scan to open

Every poster embeds a **QR code labeled "SCAN TO OPEN"** that points at the original link, so anyone who sees your card — on screen or on paper — can jump straight to the page.

### Save or share

When the card looks right:

- **Save image** — writes the poster to your download folder.
- **Share image** — sends it to any app via the system share sheet.

## Standalone QR code

Don't need a full poster? **Generate QR code** produces a simple, scannable code for the link that you can save or share on its own.

## Share as text

You can also share a link as plain text. In [Settings](./settings#shared-text), choose whether the shared text includes the **page title** and **description** before the URL, or just the URL itself. **Copy text & link** puts the same composition on your clipboard.
